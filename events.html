Programming and Events

Our programming and events are centered on creating a welcoming environment, as well as highlighting and celebrating our diversity.

Latinx Heritage Month

Celebrate Latinx Heritage Month with us every year from September 15th to October 15th! Big events during the month include our Día de los Muertos event featuring ofrendas and sugar skull painting, as well as a fun "Welcome Back" party at the start of the school year!

State of Latinx

State of Latinx is an annual event held during Latinx Heritage Month that spotlights the culture, stories, and experiences of the Latinx community. Join us as we bring together campus leaders to engage in critical dialogue around what it means to be Latinx at Cal Poly.

Día de los Muertos

Día de los Muertos falls during Latinx Heritage Month as well, and we have an all-day celebration to allow students and community members to honor their loved ones. We reserve the University Union stage for students to build ofrendas, and we also make and paint sugar skulls and enjoy some Pan de Muerto.

Cafecito con Pan Dulce

SLO doesn't have any panaderías, and we missed the experience of sitting together with our favorite pan dulce and a hot coffee. We thought you might feel the same, so once a month we team up with our friends at the Native American and Indigenous Cultural Center to bring it to you on-campus!

Monthly Mental Health Check-In

Once a month, we are joined at La CASA by Dr. Ana Cabezas from Campus Health & Wellbeing. She is a great resource, and she is available if you need any help with managing stress, anxiety, or other mental health issues. If you just want to chat, she's here for that too!
